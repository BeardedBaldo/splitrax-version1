About

Splitrax is a web application that can split your audio tracks into multi-tracks or stems. Currently, it supports splitting outputs for 2 stems(karaoke) - vocals + instrumental, 4 stems - vocals, bass, drums and others, and 5 stems - vocals, bass, drums, piano and others. The splitting is achieved by using a machine learning model called Spleeter, developed by the folks at Deezer. Check them out here - spleeter . The splitting quality is not perfect, but it is one of the best models, if not the best model out there in terms of splitting audio tracks into stems. So, have at it and see for yourself.
